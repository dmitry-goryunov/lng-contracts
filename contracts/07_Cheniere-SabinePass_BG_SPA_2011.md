LNG SALE AND PURCHASE AGREEMENT

THIS LNG SALE AND PURCHASE AGREEMENT is made and entered into as of October 25, 2011 (the “Effective
Date”), by and between Sabine Pass Liquefaction, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company whose principal place of
business is located at 700 Milam St., Suite 800, Houston, TX 77002 (“Seller”), and BG Gulf Coast LNG, LLC, a
Delaware limited liability company whose principal place of business is located at 811 Main St., Suite 3400, Houston,
TX 77002 (“Buyer”). Buyer and Seller are each referred to herein as a “Party” and collectively as the “Parties”.

Recitals

(1)   Seller's Affiliate, Sabine Pass LNG, L.P. (“SPLNG”) owns and operates a liquefied natural gas receiving and
      regasification terminal situated on the Pass Channel of the Sabine Neches Waterway in southwest Cameron
      Parish, Louisiana;

(2)   Seller is developing and intends to construct and own the Sabine Liquefaction Facility (including up to four (4)
      modules, each capable of producing approximately four decimal two (4.2) million metric tonnes per annum of
      LNG production capacity), which will be operated simultaneously with a portion of the currently existing LNG
      import capacity at the Sabine Pass Facility, making the Sabine Pass Facility a bi-directional LNG import and
      export terminal;

(3)   SPLNG provides berthing, storage, regasification, and other services at the Sabine Pass Facility to Cheniere
      Energy Investments, LLC (“Cheniere Investments”), an Affiliate of Seller, pursuant to that certain Amended and
      Restated LNG Terminal Use Agreement (as amended, the “TUA”) between Cheniere Investments and SPLNG
      dated November 9, 2006, and Cheniere Investments intends to convey, transfer or assign some or all of its rights
      under the TUA to Seller, and Seller intends to take such conveyance, transfer, or assignment of such rights, in
      order to enable Seller to utilize certain of SPLNG's facilities and provide services under this Agreement;

(4)   Buyer is or may be engaged in the purchase of LNG at the Sabine Pass Facility and transportation of such LNG to
      one or more Discharge Terminals; and

(5)   Seller and Buyer desire to execute a definitive agreement setting out the Parties' respective rights and obligations
      in relation to the sale and purchase of LNG.
